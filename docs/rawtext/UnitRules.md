# Unit Special Movement Rules
## Infantry
Move 1 space; stop when entering hostile territory

## Tanks
Move 2 spaces; can blitz through unoccupied hostile territory

## Mechanized Infantry
Move 2 spaces; can blitz with tank (one per tank)

## Artillery
Move 2 non-combat spaces if starting and ending in the same territory as a Mechanized Infantry (one artillery per Mechanized Infantry). 

## Fighters
Move 4 spaces (5 from air base); must reserve movement for landing

## Tactical Bombers
Move 4 spaces (5 from air base); must reserve movement for landing

## Strategic Bombers
Move 6 spaces (7 from air base); must reserve movement for landing

## Sea Units
Move 2 spaces (3 from naval base); stop when entering hostile sea zone

## Submarines
Can move through enemy ships (except destroyers); stop at enemy destroyer


# Unit Special Rules
## Kamikaze Strikes
Kamikaze strikes hit on an attack roll of 2 or less. If a hit is scored, it must be applied to the chosen unit.

## Sunk Capital Ships
Battleship, and Aircraft Carrier are worth 1 IPC per sunk ship.

## Destroyer Enhancement - Naval mines
Each destroyer can during the purchasing phase lay 1 Naval mine for 2 IPC each, in the sea zone they are currently in.
Restriction 1: Only 1 Naval mine can be present in each per faction (Axis/Allies).
Restriction 2: You can only have a number of Naval mines on the board up to or equivalent to the number of destroyers your nation is having.

## Naval mine
Each time an enemy sea unit makes a move into a sea zone with a Naval mine, the player has to roll a die. If a 1 is rolled, they must immediately take a hit on a ship of his/her choosing. If a 2-6 is rolled, the mine remains.

## Strategic Bombers
### Combat Limitations
Strategic bombers can only roll dice or be claimed as casualties during the first round of combat. After the first round, surviving bombers are set aside as if they had retreated.

### Undefended Transports
Strategic bombers no longer auto-kill undefended transports. Instead, roll one die per bomber and kill one transport per roll of 4 or less.

### Paratrooper Delivery - Combat Move
Each strategic bomber may deliver up to 2 infantry as paratroopers without technology. Both the strategic bomber and infantry must begin movement in the same territory.
 - Must invade with at least 1 land unit per infantry delivered
 - Strategic bombers do not roll combat dice when delivering paratroopers
 - Strategic bombers trigger Anti-aircraft Artillery and may be hit - cargo dies if the strategic bomber is hit

### Paratrooper Delivery - Noncombat Move
Strategic bombers that didn't participate in combat may deliver paratroopers during noncombat move.
Destination must have at least 1 friendly faction land unit per infantry delivered
Strategic bomber may land in destination or continue flying with remaining fuel

## Air Transports
Strategic bombers, and Heavy bombers can be reclassified as a transport plane, or a cargo plane.
Strategic bombers may only be used as a transport plane.
Heavy bomber may be used as either a transport plane, or a cargo plane.
Transport plane has move 6, attack 0, defend 0, Capacity 2 Infantry
Cargo Plane has move 6, attack 0, defend 0, Capacity 1 unit

## Cruiser Enhancements - AA
Cruisers have a built in anti-aircraft gun, which can fire on up to 2 aircraft prior to a battle.

## Fighter Enhancement - Intercept
Fighters gain the Intercept ability in all combats - with the capability of hitting other aircraft, not only when defending tactical/strategic bombing raids.
