# Research
## Setup
Each Major power chooses a technology and places roundel upside down in phase 1 free of charge. Minor powers, Italy, and ANZAC, must wait till their turn to invest in Research and Development if desired.

## Research and Development Phase
 - Pay 4 IPC's and place tech token in phase 1.
 - Phases 1 and 2 cost 4 IPC's to advance.
 - P-3 (ROLL), player rolls 1 die and pays amount rolled, if a 6 is rolled, advancement is immediately in effect, turn over marker and place in an empty slot, otherwise move marker to "P4" and on next turn player may purchase as many dice as wanted for 1 IPC each, in hopes to roll a 5 or 6.
 - Only one tech may be in development at any time.
 - Development may be abandoned at any time with all investment lost.

## Breakthrough Technology

### Self Propelled Artillery
Artillery now supports 2 Infantry or Mechanized Infantry, in addition moves 2, up from 1.

### Super Battleships
Battleships now roll 2 dice on attacks and bombardments.

### Heavy Bombers
Strategic bombers become Heavy bombers, and now roll 2 dice on attacks and bombing raids.

### Super Carriers
Carriers now support 3 planes.

### Heavy Tanks
Tanks now defend at 4.

### Improved Transports
Naval transports can now carry any 2 ground units, plus 1 Infantry.

### Super Submarines
Submarine attack increases to 3, up from 2.

### Jet Fighters
Fighter attack increases to 4, Escorting and Intercepting fighter hits are increased to 2.

### Improved Shipyards
See "Unit Statistics" chart for Improved Shipyard unit costs.

### Radar
Anti-Aircraft units, and Facility fire is increased to 2, Air Bases can scramble unlimited planes, up from 3.

### Long Range Aircraft
All air unit range is increased by two spaces.
