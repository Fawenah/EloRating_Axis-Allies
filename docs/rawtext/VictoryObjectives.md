# Victory Objectives

This document is a normalized reference for objective tracking. Each objective is listed with:
- **Condition** (when it can be evaluated)
- **Requirement** (full requirement text and full territory/city lists)
- **Reward / Outcome**
- **Notes** (if present)

## Major Objectives

### Axis Major Objectives

1. **Supremacy**
   - **Condition:** Major objective check.
   - **Requirement:** Control Washington and/or San Francisco.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** Counts as one Axis major objective.

2. **London**
   - **Condition:** Major objective check.
   - **Requirement:** Control London and/or convoy disrupt a total of 12 IPC in one turn from originally owned UK Europe territories in their Collect Income phase.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** Counts as one Axis major objective.

3. **Sydney**
   - **Condition:** Major objective check.
   - **Requirement:** Control Sydney.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** Counts as one Axis major objective.

4. **Moscow**
   - **Condition:** Major objective check.
   - **Requirement:** Control Moscow.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** Counts as one Axis major objective.

5. **China**
   - **Condition:** Major objective check.
   - **Requirement:** Control all 18 originally Chinese territories: Kansu, Tsinghai, Sikang, Kiangsu, Shantung, Hopei, Jehol, Kweichow, Hunan, Kiangsi, Yunnan, Anhwe, Chahar, Suiyuyan, Shensi, Szechwan, Kwangsi, Manchuria.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** Counts as one Axis major objective.

6. **Europe**
   - **Condition:** Major objective check.
   - **Requirement:** Control **7** victory cities on the Europe side of the map from this full list of 12 cities: Washington, Ottawa, Johannisburg, Cairo, London, Moscow, Leningrad, Stalingrad, Warsaw, Berlin, Rome, Paris.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** Counts as one Axis major objective.

7. **Pacific**
   - **Condition:** Major objective check.
   - **Requirement:** Control all 8 originally American islands: Philippines, Guam, Wake Island, Johnston Island, Hawaiian Islands, Midway, Line Islands, Aleutian Islands.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** Counts as one Axis major objective.

8. **Africa**
   - **Condition:** Major objective check.
   - **Requirement:** Control Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia, Libya, Tobruk, Alexandria, Egypt.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** Counts as one Axis major objective.

9. **Asia**
   - **Condition:** Major objective check.
   - **Requirement:** Control Calcutta, Malaya, Hong Kong, Shanghai.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** Counts as one Axis major objective.

10. **Economy**
    - **Condition:** Major objective check.
    - **Requirement:** Control territories worth a combined total of 144 IPCs.
    - **Reward / Outcome:** Counts as one Axis major objective.

7. **Asia**
   - **Condition:** Major objective check.
   - **Requirement:** Control Calcutta, Malaya, Hong Kong, Shanghai.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** Counts as one Allied major objective.

8. **Occupation**
   - **Condition:** Major objective check.
   - **Requirement:** Control Poland, Bulgaria, Finland, and Iraq.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** Counts as one Allied major objective.

9. **Diplomacy**
   - **Condition:** Major objective check.

    - **Condition:** End of game.
    - **Requirement:** Score is tied at end of game.
    - **Reward / Outcome:** Allies win automatically.

## National Objectives (Bonus Income / Units)

### Germany — "Lebensraum"

#### Condition Group: Germany is **not** at war with the Soviet Union
- **Objective:** Soviet trade access
  - **Requirement:** Germany is not at war with the Soviet Union.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs representing wheat and oil from the Soviet Union.

#### Condition Group: Germany is at war with the Soviet Union
- **Objective:** Key Soviet cities
  - **Requirement:** Germany controls each listed territory.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs **for each** of: Novgorod (Leningrad), Volgograd (Stalingrad), Russia (Moscow).
- **Objective:** Caucasus access
  - **Requirement:** An Axis power controls Caucasus.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.

#### Condition Group: Germany is at war with the United Kingdom and France
- **Objective:** Egypt land presence
  - **Requirement:** At least 1 German land unit is in Axis-controlled Egypt.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.
- **Objective:** Denmark and Norway control
  - **Requirement:** Germany controls both Denmark and Norway, and Sweden is neither pro-Allies nor Allies-controlled.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.
- **Objective:** Middle East territories
  - **Requirement:** Germany controls each listed territory.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +2 IPCs **for each** of: Iraq, Persia, Northwest Persia.
- **Objective:** Submarine pressure
  - **Requirement:** At least 5 submarines are on the map (excluding Baltic sea zones 113, 114, 115) during Germany's Collect Income phase after deploying reinforcements.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.

### Soviet Union — "The Great Patriotic War"

#### Condition Group: Soviet Union is at war on the European side
- **Objective:** Capture Berlin (one-time)
  - **Requirement:** First time the Soviet Union controls Germany (Berlin).
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +10 IPCs (one time only).
- **Objective:** Eastern Europe control block
  - **Requirement:** Soviet Union controls **3 out of 4** from this full list: Finland, Poland, Slovakia/Hungary, Romania.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +10 IPCs.
- **Objective:** No foreign Allied units in original Soviet territory
  - **Requirement:** No Allied (non-Soviet Union) units are on any originally controlled Soviet territories.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.
- **Objective:** Archangel reinforcement
  - **Requirement:** Soviet Union controls Archangel and Allies control Scotland and Iceland.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** 1 Artillery in Archangel.
- **Objective:** Caucasus reinforcement
  - **Requirement:** Soviet Union controls Caucasus and Allies control Persia and Northwest Persia.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** 1 Artillery in Caucasus.

#### Condition Group: Soviet Union is at war on the Pacific side
- **Objective:** Amur reinforcement
  - **Requirement:** Soviet Union controls Amur and Allies control Aleutian Islands and Alaska.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** 1 Artillery in Amur.

  - **Reward / Outcome:** +3 IPC **for each** of: Guam, Midway, Wake Island, Gilbert Islands, Solomon Islands, Fiji, Philippines, Johnston Island, Line Islands, Hawaiian Islands.
- **Objective:** Burma
  - **Requirement:** Axis powers control Burma.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.
- **Objective:** Central Pacific chain
  - **Requirement:** Axis powers control all of: Guam, Midway, Wake Island, Gilbert Islands, Solomon Islands.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.
- **Objective:** Key capital territories
  - **Requirement:** Axis powers control each listed territory.

- **Objective:** Dutch East Indies block
  - **Requirement:** Axis powers control all of: Sumatra, Java, Borneo, Celebes.
  - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.

### United Kingdom Europe — National Objectives

- **Maximum UK Europe bonus:** 20 IPCs per turn.

1. **British Colonies**
   - **Condition:** UK Europe objective check.
   - **Requirement:** UK Europe controls all its original territories within Africa.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.
   - **Notes:** Required territories include Egypt, Anglo-Egyptian Sudan, Union of South Africa, and all other original UK territories in Africa.

2. **British Commonwealth**
   - **Condition:** UK Europe objective check.
   - **Requirement:** UK Europe controls all its original territories outside Africa.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.
   - **Notes:** Required territories include UK itself, Scotland, Iceland, Gibraltar, Malta, and Canadian territories (Ontario, Quebec, New Brunswick/Nova Scotia).

3. **Mediterranean Theater**
   - **Condition:** UK Europe objective check.
   - **Requirement:** There are no Axis warships in the Mediterranean Sea.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.
   - **Notes:** Mediterranean includes sea zones 92 through 99. Requirement is zero Axis warships in these zones.

4. **European Theater**
   - **Condition:** UK Europe objective check.
   - **Requirement:** At least 1 UK Europe land unit is on an originally controlled French territory in Europe.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.
   - **Notes:** The listed French territories are France, Normandy-Bordeaux, and Southern France.

### United Kingdom Pacific — National Objectives

- **Maximum UK Pacific bonus:** 20 IPCs per turn.

1. **Hong Kong**
   - **Condition:** UK Pacific is at war with Japan.
   - **Requirement:** UK Pacific controls Hong Kong.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.

2. **Malaya**
   - **Condition:** UK Pacific is at war with Japan.
   - **Requirement:** UK Pacific controls Malaya.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.

3. **Shanghai**
   - **Condition:** UK Pacific is at war with Japan.
   - **Requirement:** Allies control Shanghai (any Allied power).
   - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.
   - **Notes:** China or UK Pacific typically controls Shanghai.

4. **Outer Perimeter**
   - **Condition:** UK Pacific is at war with Japan.
   - **Requirement:** UK Pacific has at least 1 land unit on Sumatra, Java, Celebes, and/or Dutch New Guinea.
   - **Reward / Outcome:** +5 IPCs.
   - **Notes:** Only one of these islands is required for this objective.
